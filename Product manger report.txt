# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Report

**Role: Product Manager Perspective**

---

## 📌 1. Problem Statement

Customer churn is a critical issue for subscription-based businesses, especially in the telecom industry. High churn rates directly impact revenue, customer lifetime value, and growth.

**Objective:**
Identify key factors driving customer churn and recommend actionable strategies to improve retention.

---

## 🎯 2. Business Goals

* Reduce customer churn rate
* Improve customer retention
* Increase customer lifetime value (CLV)
* Identify high-risk customer segments

---

## 📂 3. Data Overview

The dataset contains customer-level information including:

* Demographics (Gender, Senior Citizen)
* Subscription details (Contract type, Internet service)
* Billing information (Monthly Charges, Total Charges)
* Customer behavior (Tenure, Payment Method)
* Target variable: **Churn (Yes/No)**

---

## 🔍 4. Key Insights

### 🔹 1. Contract Type Drives Churn

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show the highest churn rate
* Long-term contracts (1 year / 2 year) have significantly lower churn

👉 **Insight:** Lack of commitment increases churn risk

---

### 🔹 2. High Monthly Charges Increase Churn

* Customers paying higher monthly fees are more likely to churn

👉 **Insight:** Price sensitivity is a major factor

---

### 🔹 3. Tenure Matters

* Customers with **short tenure** churn more frequently
* Long-term customers are more loyal

👉 **Insight:** Early-stage retention is critical

---

### 🔹 4. Payment Method Influence

* Certain payment methods (e.g., electronic check) show higher churn

👉 **Insight:** Payment friction may impact retention

---

### 🔹 5. Demographics (Minor Impact)

* Gender and senior citizen status show **less significant impact** compared to contract and pricing

---

## 📊 5. Dashboard Summary

The Power BI dashboard provides:

* KPI metrics (Total Customers, Churn Count, Churn Rate)
* Contract-based churn analysis
* Customer segmentation (Gender, Senior Citizen)
* Revenue indicators (Monthly Charges)
* Behavioral insights (Tenure, Payment Method)

---

## 🚀 6. Recommendations (Product Strategy)

### 🔹 1. Promote Long-Term Contracts

* Offer discounts for yearly plans
* Introduce loyalty rewards

---

### 🔹 2. Optimize Pricing Strategy

* Provide flexible pricing tiers
* Offer bundled services

---

### 🔹 3. Improve Early Customer Experience

* Focus on onboarding
* Provide proactive support in first 3 months

---

### 🔹 4. Simplify Payment Experience

* Improve payment reliability
* Offer incentives for auto-pay

---

### 🔹 5. Target High-Risk Customers

* Use churn prediction models
* Send retention offers

---

## 📈 7. Product Impact

If implemented, these strategies can:

* Reduce churn rate by 10–20%
* Increase customer retention
* Improve long-term revenue growth

---

## 🧠 8. Future Scope

* Build ML model for churn prediction
* Personalize offers using customer segmentation
* Integrate real-time analytics

---

## ✅ Conclusion

This analysis highlights that **contract type, pricing, and tenure** are the primary drivers of churn.

A data-driven product strategy focusing on **customer experience, pricing optimization, and retention campaigns** can significantly improve business outcomes.
